Siga Majvest 200

Siga Majvest 200 is a mechanically attached membrane that serves as both the water resistive barrier (WRB) and the air barrier. Majvest comprises two high strength spun-bonded polypropylene/fleece layers that provide durability unparalleled to generic house wrap (such as tyvek) and an inner polymeric layer that is highly vapor-permeable while remaining water tight.

Storage

Store in original packaging in a cool, dry location. Protect from direct sunlight and weather. No long-term storage limitations.

Detailing

SIt is important to properly detail transitions between (substrates, draingage planes, structural assemblies, cladding, foundation, roof systems), openings, penetrations, and fasteners to ensure the moist continuous WRB/AB system. See installation guide sections 4.5-5.3 for detailed instructions for each scenario. In short, layer bottom up, with each additional layer being installed ON TOP of previous layer in order to shed water efficiently. Transition with metal flashing and tape all seams with wigluv 60. For foundation detailing use a combination of Fentrim 430 Grey, Wigluv 60 and hemmed SS drip edge flashing.
