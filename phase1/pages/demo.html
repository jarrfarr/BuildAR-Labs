CMHC Accessory Dwelling Unit 01

About This Project

The CMHC Housing Design Catalogue is a federal initiative created to speed up housing delivery across Canada by providing pre-designed, code-aware, adaptable residential building templates. The catalogue focuses on missing-middle and infill housing—ADUs, multiplexes, and small apartment forms—designed to be cost-efficient, accessible, and easy to adapt to local zoning and site conditions. Accessory Dwelling Unit 01 is one example: a standardized, single-storey backyard home of roughly 59 m² (634 ft²) with 1 bedroom and 1 bath, laid out for accessible living and efficient construction.
