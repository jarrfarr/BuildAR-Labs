About BuildAR (Beta)

Hi, I'm Jarryd. I started developing BuildAR after a discussion in class about a common problem in residential construction: most mistakes happen because 2D plans are confusing or easy to misread.

We began building the platform with guidance from my instructors, Shande and Connor, and collaboration with a few of my peers. Our focus was clear: create a tool that improves efficiency, enhances communication, and is easy to use, while applying principles of building science to residential projects.

BuildAR delivers step-by-step installation guides, interactive 3D models, and AR-enhanced workflows that reduce errors, save time, and help projects run smoothly.

We are now seeking funding and welcome partnerships with architects, builders, trade professionals, and manufacturers who want to advance AR technology in residential construction.
